Customer Demographics Study 📊

**Introduction**

The Customer Demographics Study project focuses on analyzing customer demographic data to understand customer behavior, preferences, and purchasing patterns. Demographic analysis helps businesses identify target audiences and make better marketing and business decisions.

This project uses Python and data analytics techniques to study customer information such as age, gender, income, location, and spending behavior. The collected insights help organizations improve customer engagement and business strategies.


**Project Objectives 🎯**

- To analyze customer demographic data
- To identify customer behavior patterns
- To understand customer segmentation
- To study age, gender, and income distribution
- To visualize customer insights using charts and graphs
- To support data-driven business decisions



**Problem Statement ❓**

Businesses often collect large amounts of customer data but face difficulty understanding customer characteristics and purchasing behavior. Without proper analysis, companies may fail to target the right audience effectively.

This project solves the problem by analyzing demographic data and converting it into meaningful visual insights that help businesses improve marketing strategies and customer satisfaction.

**Project Workflow 🔄**

1. Data Collection

Customer demographic data is collected in CSV format.

2. Data Cleaning

Data preprocessing includes:

- Handling missing values
- Removing duplicate records
- Formatting columns
- Data type conversion

3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

EDA is performed to:

- Understand customer distribution
- Analyze age groups
- Study gender ratio
- Identify spending patterns

4. Data Visualization

Different visualizations are created to represent customer demographics clearly.

5. Insight Generation

Business insights are generated from the analyzed data.
